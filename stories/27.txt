Here are the flowers.
"The King of the Ocean," said the boy, as he saw them fall on the floor. "The King of the Ocean has come."
The child cried out, "It is too soon...it is too soon." 
And he fell. "A new king is come to the world," he said. 
There was no man who could save him. 

So the King of the Ocean came. 
"O my friends," he said, "That is for the Sea. They said the Sea might be your salvation. But when he came, he was a stranger to both men and beasts."
